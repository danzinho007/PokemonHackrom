About Some Gyms:
Baobab City Gym: Jump down from certain holes to reach those statues which are surrounded by rocks.
Miscanthus Town Gym: Defeat the divers to clean the diving spots. Find the real diving spot and emerge from the spot, you'll reach the crashed ship. Try to find the buttons in the rooms(Check out the desks, trash cans and cracked floors)to unlock the doors of those rooms.
