▬▬▬▬▬▬▬▬ RECOMMENDED EMULATORS ▬▬▬▬▬▬▬▬

Use these emulators:

PC -> mGBA 0.9.2/mGBA 0.10.2
Android -> RetroArch (mGBA core), Lemuroid
iOS -> RetroArch (mGBA core)

If the game crashes when you close the bag it's a BIOS problem of your device/emulator.
